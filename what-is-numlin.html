❓ What is Numlin?

Numlin is a secret code language made by Spark — and it's super simple to learn!

🔢 How it works:

  • Each letter of the English alphabet is turned into its position number.
  • A = 1, B = 2, ..., Z = 26
  • Numbers are separated with commas.
  • Words are separated with spaces.
  • Punctuation stays the same!

💬 Example:

Text: HELLO WORLD
Numlin: 8,5,12,12,15 23,15,18,12,4
